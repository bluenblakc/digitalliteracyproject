## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails
- Always verify website URLs before entering details
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Avoid sharing personal information online

### 💰 UPI / Financial Safety
- Never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Double-check payment requests before approving

### 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Helpline: 1930 (24x7)

- Keep your devices updated with latest security patches
- Use trusted antivirus software
